About Us




About Fire Care Engineers


Among the several Fire Fighting Engineering and Consultation Companies, We proudly acclaim ourselves as one of the leading company, since the recent years. Our potential to the overall efficiency is highly based on the strength of a team of expert engineers who provide the clients with their excellence advice on the type of Fire Fighting Systems, taking into consideration of National and International design codes and standards such as NBC, TAC, NFPA, FM etc., according to their needs.

The company maintains stringent quality standards and uncompromising commitment towards complete customer satisfaction. We have also achieved quantum growth business and have now emerged one of the leading companies in the market.

As a provider of quality service, we offer complete fire protection solutions like Consultation, surveys, Feasibility study, Supply and Installation of fire fighting equipments

We are approved by statutory authorities like Fire brigade, Jharkhand .



Our Mission


To create awareness of the essentiality attached to a firefighting system, as an important requisite in everyday life and loss





Our Expertise


We are capable of designing complex fire protection Systems such as Fire Hydrant, Sprinkler, High and Medium velocity water spray, Automatic/Manual fire detection system, Addressable fire alarm system, Co2 flooding System, Clean Agent gas based fire suppression system, Inergen System and Foam flooding system that strictly follow the requirements of the local fire services: TAC/CCE-DO or any other competent authority.

We also provide in house depending on the type of job demanded by the customers. We are rest assured that we are proficient in controlling and solving any problem in the field of Fire fighting.
